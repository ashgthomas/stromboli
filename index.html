The best in town!

We're Stromboli's Italian Eatery, and we're Starkville's favorite place for amazing friends and food. Located right outside of campus past the Hunter Henry Center, we strive to give our customers the best from the garden-to-table ingredients to the fresh dough we make in house daily. Stop by for a late lunch with friends or for a pleasant dinner with the family - between our delicious entrees and cozy atmosphere, we're sure to leave your tastebuds screaming for more.
